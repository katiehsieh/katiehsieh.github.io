Percussion

Percussion instruments consist of a variety of instruments that are generally hit to produce the sound.

Examples

  • snare drum
  • bass drum
  • xylophone
  • triangle.
  • timpani

There are a huge variety of percussion instruments.

Common string instruments that use a bow include the violin, viola, cello. The bow is a stick with hair attached to it, and the bow slides against the strings to make the sound. These bowed instruments are most often found in orchestras. The guitar, ukulele, and harp are some instruments that are usually plucked and strummed.

String instruments have different lengths and thicknesses of stretched strings. Longer and looser strings produce lower notes, while shorter and tighter strings produce higher notes. Common string instruments that use a bow include the violin, viola, cello. The bow is a stick with hair attached to it, and the bow slides against the strings to make the sound. These bowed instruments are most often found in orchestras.

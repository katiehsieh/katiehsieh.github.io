Musical Instruments

Woodwinds

Woodwind instruments are blown and most have a wooden reed that vibrates to produce the sound.

Examples

  • flute
  • clarinet
  • oboe
  • bassoon
  • saxophone
Full list

Woodwinds produce different notes by covering different holes on the instrument. Woodwind instruments also come in different sizes. The smaller instruments can produce higher notes, while the larger instruments produce the lower notes.

Some woodwind instruments do not have reeds, such as the flute and piccolo. Woodwind instruments that have a single reed include the clarinet and saxaphone. Double-reed instruments include the oboe and bassoon. These woodwind instruments are often found in orchestras, concert bands, and marching bands.
